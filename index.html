The best parts of life are lived, felt, and shared—together.

Life's biggest moments aren't experienced alone—so why capture them that way? Whether it's a wedding, your baby's first year, or a summer with friends, everything that makes it special—the photos, videos, reactions, inside jokes—ends up scattered or lost.

Moments brings it all together. Create shared spaces, invite the people who matter, and watch everyone's perspectives come together: the candid shots, the heartfelt messages, the comments that capture how it felt. The full story.

What makes Moments different

Private & Invite-Only

Your moments belong to you and the people you choose—not a public feed, not an algorithm.

Everyone Contributes

Everyone adds photos, videos, messages, and reactions—capturing what it meant from every perspective.

One Place for Everything

Every photo, video, and message lives together—organized and easy to find.

One Code to Join

No accounts to connect, no permissions to configure. Share the code and everyone's in.

Share Beyond the Group

Generate a shareable link to the full moment or just the parts you choose—no app required.

Private by Default

Your data is yours. We don't sell it, mine it, or use it to target you with ads.
