1. Introduction

People Fabric LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Moments mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our App.

By using Moments, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Account Information

  • Email address (required for account creation and authentication)
  • First and last name (to personalize your experience)
  • Profile photo (optional, to help others recognize you)

User Content

  • Photos and videos you upload to Moments
  • Text messages and comments you post
  • Reactions you add to content

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Device Information

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system and version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • App version

Usage Information

  • Features you use and how you interact with the App
  • Time spent in the App
  • Error logs and performance data

We use Mixpanel, an analytics service, to collect and analyze usage information. This helps us understand how people use Moments so we can improve the App. Mixpanel's privacy policy can be found at https://mixpanel.com/legal/privacy-policy/.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Browsing history outside the App
  • Financial information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide the Service: Create your account, authenticate you, and enable you to create and participate in Moments
  • Store Your Content: Securely store the photos, videos, and messages you share
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to fix bugs, improve performance, and develop new features
  • Communicate with You: Send important updates about your account or the service
  • Ensure Safety: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or violations of our Terms of Service

We do NOT use your information to:

  • Display targeted advertisements
  • Build advertising profiles
  • Sell to third parties
  • Track you across other apps or websites

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 With Other Users

When you participate in a Moment, other participants can see:

  • Your name and profile photo
  • Content you post to that Moment
  • Your reactions and comments

You control which Moments you join and what content you share.

4.2 With Service Providers

We work with trusted service providers who help us operate the App:

  • Supabase: Authentication and file storage
  • MongoDB Atlas: Database storage
  • Mixpanel: Analytics

These providers only access your data as necessary to perform their services and are contractually obligated to protect your information.

4.3 When Required by Law

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests by public authorities.

4.4 What We Do NOT Do

  • We do NOT sell your personal information
  • We do NOT share your data with advertisers
  • We do NOT share your data with data brokers
  • We do NOT share your data for marketing purposes

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Where We Store Your Data

Your data is stored on secure cloud servers provided by our service partners (Supabase and MongoDB Atlas) located in the United States.

5.2 How We Protect Your Data

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption of data at rest
  • Secure authentication using one-time passcodes
  • Regular security assessments

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your information for as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: When you delete your account, your personal data and content are deleted immediately from our servers
  • Anonymous Analytics: Aggregated, anonymous analytics data may be retained for longer periods

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Download Your Data

You can access your account information through the App. You can download your content (photos, videos, messages) before deleting your account.

7.2 Update Your Information

You can update your profile information (name, profile photo) at any time through the App.

7.3 Delete Your Account

You can delete your account at any time through the App's settings. Upon deletion:

  • Your account is immediately deactivated
  • Your personal data is permanently deleted
  • Your content is removed from our servers

7.4 Opt-Out of Analytics

You can contact us at privacy@peoplefabric.com to request that we exclude your data from analytics collection.

8. Children's Privacy

Moments is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 16, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

If you believe we may have collected information from a child under 16, please contact us at privacy@peoplefabric.com.

9. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside the United States, please be aware that your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States, where our servers are located and our central database is operated.

9.1 European Union Users

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

Your Rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: Request limitation of processing
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing

Legal Basis for Processing:

  • Contract: Processing necessary to provide you with the service
  • Legitimate Interests: Processing necessary for our legitimate business interests (such as improving the App), where those interests are not overridden by your rights
  • Consent: Where you have given explicit consent

To exercise your GDPR rights, contact us at privacy@peoplefabric.com.
